## Product decision

Bridge-Maker is now framed as a contract-first QA/RL framework:

- primary path: lightweight decorators/attributes over existing game or adapter functions;
- reverse-project path: external adapters such as a NoitaRL bridge;
- advanced path: CE/Ghidra/VLM assist for missing fields/actions.

The strongest existing stack remains: state maps, env generation, Ray/RLlib + ICM, dashboard, Oracle, reports. The fragile part, fully automatic semantic discovery from arbitrary binaries, is now optional assist tooling rather than the product promise.
